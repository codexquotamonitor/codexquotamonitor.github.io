Codex Quota Monitor

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

Codex Quota Monitor is a browser extension that displays your Codex usage quota directly in the browser toolbar.

🔒 We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your device.
🔓 This extension is open source. You can inspect every line of code at github.com/codexquotamonitor/codex-quota-monitor and verify our privacy practices yourself.

What data is accessed

The extension reads the following data exclusively from ChatGPT's own usage endpoint, using your existing authenticated browser session:

  • Your current Codex usage limits
  • Session quota usage percentage and reset time (5-hour window)
  • Weekly quota usage percentage and reset time (7-day window)
  • Any extra Codex credits available beyond your plan limits
  • Your account plan type (e.g. Pro)

This data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in chrome.storage.local API and is never sent anywhere else.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not read your conversations or any Codex content
  • We do not store data beyond what is necessary to display your quota

Permissions explained

  • storage — saves quota data locally on your device
  • tabs — opens chatgpt.com when you click the "View quota" button
  • alarms — schedules background refresh every 10 minutes
  • host permission (chatgpt.com) — reads the usage API using your existing session cookies

Third-party services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs other than ChatGPT's own usage endpoint, which you are already authenticated with.

Children's privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
